# Decision Framework

Intelligence Hub uses this flow when turning information into recommendations:

```text
Observe
Collect
Verify
Abstract
Connect
Reason
Score
Recommend
Reflect
```

## Observe

Identify the raw event or source item.

## Collect

Gather supporting context from available connectors and memory.

## Verify

Check whether the source is credible, repeated, primary, or speculative.

## Abstract

Extract the underlying technology, concept, market force, or behavioral pattern.

## Connect

Relate the item to papers, repositories, companies, technologies, timelines, and prior memory.

## Reason

Explain why the item matters and what could follow.

## Score

Assign an Intelligence Score based on weighted dimensions:

- Importance
- Impact
- Momentum
- Engineering Value
- Adoption
- Longevity
- Novelty

## Recommend

Choose one action:

- Ignore
- Watch
- Read
- Prototype
- Implement
- Review later

## Reflect

After time passes, compare the recommendation with what actually happened.
